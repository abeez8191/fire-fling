Help

Quick Start

  1. Tap the black fling icon at the top of the screen to connect to your Fire device
  2. Select your device from the pop-up
  3. Find your video by either entering a search term or url in the URL bar
  4. When a video is detected, tap on the link to fling the video to your TV
  5. To disconnect from your Fire device, tap on the light blue icon at the top

Troubleshooting

If you don't see an icon at the top, the app cannot find an Amazon Fire device. Please make sure that your device is plugged in and has power.
